About Lucky Please

Free online games for everyday "who pays, who goes, who's it" moments.

What We Do

Lucky Please is a collection of free, instantly-playable random decision games. No sign-up, no download — open a link, add names, spin or tap, done. Built for the small everyday decisions that shouldn't need a committee meeting: who pays for coffee, who picks dinner, who's on whose team, who loses at rock-paper-scissors five times in a row and has to do the thing.

The Games

Wheel Spinner

Classic roulette wheel. Add names, spin, land on one.

Ladder (Amidakuji)

Korean/Japanese ghost leg — pick a line, follow the path.

🏎️ Car Racing

Watch your name race to the finish.

🎱 Lotto

Draw numbered balls, Korean-style lottery.

Team Picker

Split a group into balanced teams instantly.

Why Lucky Please

  • Zero friction: Works on phone and desktop, no install, no login required.
  • 16 languages: Korean, English, Japanese, Chinese, Spanish, German, French, Portuguese, Russian, Arabic, Hindi, Thai, Indonesian, Vietnamese, Turkish — and growing.
  • Shareable: Results encode into a URL so your group can see the same outcome.
  • Free, forever: Ad-supported. No paywall, no premium gates on the core games.

무엇을 하는 사이트인가요

Lucky Please는 무료로 즉시 이용 가능한 랜덤 의사결정 게임 모음입니다. 가입도, 다운로드도 필요 없습니다 — 링크만 열고 이름 넣고 돌리면 끝. 소소한 일상 결정을 위한 도구입니다: 커피 내기, 밥 먹을 곳 정하기, 팀 나누기, 가위바위보에 다섯 번 진 사람이 할 일 정하기.

Lucky Please를 쓰는 이유

  • 설치·가입 0단계: 모바일·PC에서 바로 실행. 로그인은 선택.
