Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026

1. Who We Are

Lucky Please (luckyplz.com) is a free online games website that provides random decision tools — wheel spinners, ladder games, car racing, lotto, and team pickers — primarily used to decide who pays for coffee, picks teams, or faces a light penalty.

2. What Data We Collect

  • Usage analytics: We use Google Analytics 4 (measurement ID G-NZDPE3H3DQ) to collect pageviews, country, device type, and anonymized IP. We do not collect personally identifiable information through analytics.
  • Advertising cookies: We use Google AdSense to serve ads. Google may use the DART cookie to show personalized ads based on your activity across Google services and third-party sites. You can opt out at adssettings.google.com.
  • Account data (optional): If you sign in with Google, your email and display name are stored in Supabase so you can save groups and sync settings across devices. Sign-in is optional — all core games work without an account.
  • Browser local storage: Your language preference, saved groups, and game settings are stored in your browser's local storage. This data never leaves your device unless you sign in.

3. Cookies

We and our partners use cookies for:

  • Essential: Language preference, session state
  • Analytics: Google Analytics 4 (_ga, _gid)
  • Advertising: Google AdSense (including the DART cookie) for personalized ads. For EEA, UK, and Swiss visitors, we use Google's Consent Management Platform to obtain consent before loading personalized advertising cookies.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdSense — Advertising Policies
  • Google Analytics — Privacy Policy
  • Supabase — Privacy Policy (database & authentication)
  • Cloudflare — Privacy Policy (hosting & CDN)

5. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

If you are in the European Economic Area, United Kingdom, Switzerland, or California, you have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete data we hold about you
  • Withdraw consent to personalized advertising at any time
  • Object to processing or request data portability
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

To exercise these rights, email us at the address below.

6. Data Retention

Account data (if you sign in) is retained as long as your account exists. Google Analytics 4 retains event-level data for 14 months. Advertising cookies follow Google's standard retention policies.

7. Children's Privacy

Lucky Please is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us for removal.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the latest version.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions, requests, or complaints, email luckyplz.contact@gmail.com.
